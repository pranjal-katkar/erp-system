# Scope of ERP Mini-System

## In-Scope

- HR Module: Employee records, departments, roles
- Inventory Module: Items, suppliers, stock quantity
- Purchase Orders: Create/update/approve orders
- Stock Control: Stock in/out, adjustments
- Finance & Expenses: Log expenses, link to PO
- RBAC: Users, roles, permission matrix

## Out of Scope

- Payroll
- Attendance
- Tax/GST integration
- Mobile app
- Analytics dashboards
